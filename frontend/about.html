From Roots to Branches

Established in 1979 by Shivdas Patel, we are a trusted timber trading business with over 45 years of experience in the industry. Our journey has been built on strong foundations of quality, transparency, and ethical business practices, enabling us to develop long-standing relationships with our customers. Guided by principles of honesty, consistency, and product integrity, we combine deep industry knowledge with a modern, professional approach to operations. Through structured processes, clear documentation, and efficient systems, we ensure reliability in every transaction. With decades of experience and a forward-looking mindset, we continue to deliver dependable quality, fair pricing, and professional service‚Äîmaking us a preferred partner for traders, contractors, and businesses who value trust and long-term association
