About Us

Established in 1979 by Shivdas Patel, we are a trusted timber trading business with over 45 years of experience. Built on integrity, quality, and transparent practices, we focus on long-term relationships and consistent delivery. Combining deep industry knowledge with modern systems, we ensure reliable operations and dependable service.
